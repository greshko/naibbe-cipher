qi alak for tonfis du
zhayl sik inim morh
altin frist por usalku
pin waalii qi si untoz
waalu cut hsokk yugai
leit hiim qidal altofra
mingot sur inim talq
siin waalii tul du hsall
talq frihso muganqifultillahso
friikko ting ohwe siiwewe
for pin latu azh sinoq zelloruu talq me
alltiikulaumtie fri untoz alfryozh fo r pin
qido qal intookh sadiq frantuuzh hso fralto
mindzhozh inim ohzhalta kindu qatto qi frido
hsall qi qolt men tuko qi fri van zhalyoqidomun
muun ti qolt qi altoozh intookh sii weare
fri iiko kktizho men qi lal alzima hiitto
frem qi okktu siimen sik inim dalt hsong
qidel altofra hizh morzho art sur leit alltiq
me cuzho kkozho ktolu allmina dalt qi uuztor
fraakt zlun uzho qi kkallstmor
__________

zhen qi mult talq altuzha mindozho forta
hing altofra mualtozh siig danik kktozh qi
ohive linweme falti sogo mita muganqiai
luto luuzho qi fan halt sinq orzh mizh
huzh solto elino mietii land inim hsall qi
muzhodukktozh fin fro musing attozhu
singdolo malt livaro balq sin qi haltusomazh
siin malqizhu friin da mult ohwe for la
altolo miqi qi qilazhut sii froo lada mindozh
mukktolo qi laduri sin quee sikkto zhol alak
tonfis fiqala muzhaan si duzholiddo friqqalt
qi maltizhkktorkh lata am ohwe en o tilla
sik qi halt bevono cua altikkt mindo me
inqizho muvitas latuzho calt qi mindzhisazh
puin du zhayl laed mesit muhalt bolazho
ruuma ki enzho fiton
